Release date: October 1st, 2015

With stone cold fists and a titan's physique, Atlas bends rock to encrust and bulldoze through all challengers in his brawl.

Champion of elemental earth, he manifests formidable ramparts, crushing boulders, and golem brethren to rampage his stomping grounds. Atlas was unearthed in Update 17.5 (2015-10-01).
